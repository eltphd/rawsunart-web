The Artist

Lacey Rawson —
Fine Art Tattooist

I'm a recognized watercolor artist based in Dublin, Ohio, working out of a private studio inside AION Tattoo. Every tattoo I do is a collaboration — you bring the concept, I bring it to life on skin.

My work draws from fine art principles: composition, color theory, negative space. I've spent years painting before I ever picked up a tattoo machine, and that training shows in everything I make. Watercolor is my signature — painterly, expressive, built to last.

Beyond tattoos, I paint original watercolor pieces and work in ceramics. The same artistic voice runs through all of it.

What I Do

Specialties &
Styles

01
Watercolor

My signature. Vivid color washes, soft edges, movement on skin. Anchored with subtle linework so the color holds for years — not months.

02
Botanical

Flowers, leaves, vines, herbs. Organic forms with fine detail and intentional composition. Natural subjects, interpreted — never traced.

03
Abstract

Shapes, forms, and color without a literal subject. Concept-driven and conversation-led — we figure out what it means to you, then I translate it.

04
Fine Line

Delicate, precise single-needle work. Portraits, illustrative pieces, clean linework. Where detail and restraint matter equally.
